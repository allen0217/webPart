Photography Contest Infomation
Contest Duration
May 20, 2012, submission cut-off at midnight local time on May 31, 2012.

Relevancy to Topic
Photo must be taken during The 2012 DFW Dragon Boat Festival in Irving, Texas being held on May 20, 2012
Maximum of three pieces can be submitted from each contestant. Both color and black & white photos are accepted. Each photo width cannot be less than 2000 pixel (height according to its aspect ratio); electronic files must be in jpeg format and cannot be less than 1MB. Hard copies will not be accepted. Photos cannot be altered with any special effect (e.g. filters) and computerized composed; however brightness, color saturation, contrast and other basic parameters can be adjusted.

Submiting method

Email all entries to photocontest@dfwdragonboatfestival.com

Please include the following information in the entry email:
- name, address, age, day time phone number, title of each photo entry

Headline Judge -- Tim Heitman

Tim has been a freelance professional photographer for over 15 years in the DFW area. He currently works with the Dallas Stars and US Presswire. He was the assistant team photographer for the Dallas Mavericks.

He has photographed most of the major sporting championships, including the World Series, Super Bowl, Stanley Cup, MLS Cup, NBA Finals and several All-Star games and numerous College Bowl games. He has also photographed all major college teams in the region, including teams in the Big 12, SEC, CUSA and Southland conferences.

Tim’s work has been published in major magazines, and newspapers, including the New York Times, Chicago Tribune, LA Times, Sports Illustrated, ESPN Magazine, USA Today and websites like Fox Sports, ESPN and Upper Deck. His work could also be seen on billboards across the country and on Topps trading cards.

Tim has recently had an image nominated for Picture of the Year for the 2011 NFL season. And he recently did a regional Sports Illustrated cover of Adrian Peterson.
