About Marco Polo World Foundation (MPWF)

Marco Polo World Foundation is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization founded in 2007. We are a non-religious and non-partisan organization with a sole focus on the promotion of intercultural understanding. Our mission is to introduce to our community the sport of dragon boat racing,and to showcase Asian culture. We launched the Annual DFW Dragon Boat, Kite and LanternFestival as an effort to pursue our mission, and to celebrate the Asian-Pacific AmericanHeritage Month. Through this festival, we hope to bring together organizations from alldifferent sectors of the community, and encourage local, regional and international interaction.MPWF is also in the process of setting up a scholarship fund for college students who show dedication to promoting intercultural understanding.
