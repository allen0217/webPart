Volunteer Opportunities with Marco Polo World Foundation

Marco Polo World Foundation is a Public charity non-profit 501 (c) (3) organization founded in 2007. We are a non-religious and non-partisan organization. Our mission is to promote intercultural understanding by sharing and celebrating International Culture with all people of the region and across America. MPWF provides four programs:

  • Annual DFW Dragon Boat, Kite and Lantern Festival
  • Scholarship program
  • Internship program
  • Asian Culture Seminar

We would like to have YOU join us to make a difference for our foundation and enjoy the benefit of volunteering: invaluable experience of giving back, the accomplishment of making a dream come true, the improvement of organizational and personal skills and the expansion of networking and opportunities, and most important: have fun!

Available year round volunteering opportunities with MPWF include:

  • Marketing/Public Relations - Strong inter-person skills needed, marketing/business background preferred
  • Media/Communication - photography/videography skill preferred
  • Documentation - Strong written skills needed
  • Website maintenance - website development experience needed
Volunteering for Annual DFW Dragon Boat, Kite and Lantern Festival

Marco Polo World Foundation is proud to be the first to bring Annual DFW Dragon boat, Kite and Lantern festival to DFW area! Since the first launching in 2007, the festival has been growing fast and more influential every year with over 20,000 attendees in 2014. The festival has been organized and supported by invaluable volunteers who devote themselves with their passion, time and hard work. The festival won't be such a success without our volunteers. You are the key to make it happen! Come and join us to enjoy a day of fun at Lake Carolyn!

Positions Needed for the Festival Day:

  • Greeters
  • Information booth
  • Registration for dragon boat teams
  • Volunteer tent food set-up
  • Parking garage directory
  • Unloading / loading Dock helpers
  • Helpers to organize dragon boat teams
  • Stage helpers
  • Educational activities at booths
  • Trash pick-up
  • MPWF membership Registration booth
