VOLUNTEERS
This festival is a non-profit event made possible by the generous support of sponsors and is completely run by volunteers from the community. If you or your organization would like to volunteer for this event, please email us

Volunteers are required for a number of key positions, including
  • Greeters
  • Information booth
  • Registration for dragon boat teams
  • Volunteer tent food set-up
  • Parking/ unloading / loading
  • Dock helpers
  • Helpers to organize dragon boat teams
  • Stage helpers
  • Educational activities at booths
  • Trash pick-up
