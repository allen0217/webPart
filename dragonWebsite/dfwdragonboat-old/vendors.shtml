VENDORS

Vendor booths consist of a six foot table and 2 chairs in a 10x10 tent. Vendors may register for space by submitting the Vendor Form and appropriate payment.

The vendor hours are from 9 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Vendor booths for food and non-food items, including arts and crafts are available. Set-up is at 8:30 a.m. on the day of the event. IMPORTANT: Food and drink vendors may not sell water.
