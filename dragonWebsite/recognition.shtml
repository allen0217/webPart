Recognition

The Annual DFW Dragon Boat, Kite, and Lantern Festival has been recognized for its contribution to increasing understanding of the Asian community within the DFW Metroplex and beyond.



Marco Polo World Foundation was recognized with "YOU ARE ALWAYS THERE FOR US" award in the DFW Asian-American Citizens Council (DFW AACC) 14th Annual Banquet on September 6, 2014 for its continued advocacy and work in the Asian American Community and support for DFW AACC.
